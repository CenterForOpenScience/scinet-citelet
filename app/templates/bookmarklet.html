Citelet

Crowd-Sourcing the Scientific Citation Network

Help promote open science by capturing the citation information on articles you view. See below for instructions.

Chrome Extension:

Install the Citelet Chrome extension by visiting the Chrome webstore.



Once the Citelet is installed, whenever you view a scientific article you will be prompted to send the article's citation information to OSF SciNet. These crowdsourced citations will be added to the network in one click.

Bookmarklet:

Don't want to visit the Chrome webstore? Simply drag and drop the green Citelet bookmarklet Then, whenever you browse to an academic article, click the bookmarklet to send us information about the article and any articles it cites. No popups will appear to interfere with your web browsing.
